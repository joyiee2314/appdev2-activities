Case Study #1
What strategies can you implement to ensure timely updates and maintenance of your software to adapt to changes in third-party software requirements?

To ensure timely updates and maintenance of our software in response to changes in third-party software requirements, we can:
    -Set up reminders for regular checks on third-party updates.
    -Keep communication lines open with providers for advance notice.
    -Assign specific team members to handle updates promptly.
    -Test updates in a controlled environment before deploying them.
    -Document update processes for easy reference.
    -Plan resources ahead to accommodate update tasks.
    -Establish a protocol for emergency updates if needed.
